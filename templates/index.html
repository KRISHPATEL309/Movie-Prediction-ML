Movies Recommendation System

A movie recommendation system, also known as a movie recommender system, is a machine learning-based technique designed to predict or filter a user’s movie preferences by analyzing their historical behavior, ratings, watch history, and interactions. It serves as an intelligent filtering tool that anticipates and suggests movies that align with the user’s tastes and interests. These systems aim to enhance user experience by offering personalized movie recommendations, helping users discover films they might not find on their own.
